The copy.

Replies of this caliber don’t come from templates. The whole approach was built around one question: why should this specific partner stop scrolling and read?

The golden bullet

  • Subject line -- we researched each partner’s portfolio and named a relevant company in the subject. That’s the one job of a subject line: get it opened.
  • Body -- six tight blocks. Intro. Credentials. What we’re building. Where we are. The ask. The CTA. No paragraphs longer than three lines.

The structure we sent

Note: the names and companies in the example below are illustrative placeholders, not Sute’s real founder or product details. We’re showing the structure -- subject-line research, six tight body blocks, no fluff -- not the literal copy.

Introduction
Hey Paul, I know this email might catch you off-guard.
I’m Kareem -- Founder of ADA (ex-Apple, I led Apple’s VR program with Dr. Steinberger. Scaled it from X to Y).
What we’re doing & significant achievements
ADA is building the VR machines that Robots and AI can access. As of today, we have secured contracts with Apple, MSFT, and other notable players.
Where we stand as of today
As of today, our revenue numbers are 50K MRR, growing at X% QoQ.
What’s our ask
Hans Capital is leading the round and we’re looking to raise $9M (SAFE) closing round in the next 60 days.
Before we speak, mind if I send over the deck?
Best,
Kareem | CEO
