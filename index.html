Most outbound looks busy.
Almost none of it works.

Three failure modes show up in nearly every audit. They kill pipeline before a single rep picks up the phone.

01 · Deliverability
0%

Your emails are landing in spam.

Without warmed infrastructure -- SPF, DKIM, DMARC, custom tracking domains -- even great copy never gets seen. Reputation tanks in 48 hours and stays tanked.

02 · ICP reach
0%
Bounce rate on raw database pulls

You can't actually reach your ICP.

Public databases like Apollo and ZoomInfo go stale fast. High bounces burn sender reputation. The buyers you actually want stay invisible behind verification gaps.

"Hope this finds you well." Templated openers, generic value props, no positioning. Without sharp message-market fit, replies stay below 2% no matter the sending volume.
